A art of folding paper into decorative shapes and figures.


Origami is fun and loads of people love it. During this lockdown everyone are origami lover/craft lover. Origami helps develop hand-eye coordination, fine motor skills, and mental concentration. The use of the hands directly stimulates areas of the brain. Origami is used in various therapeutic settings, including art therapy and in stroke and injury rehabilitation.
